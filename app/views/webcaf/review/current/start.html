Review a WebCAF self-assessment

Use this service to review an organisation’s WebCAF self-assessment against National Cyber Security Centre's Cyber Assurance Framework (CAF) (opens in new tab).

You will be able to:

  • confirm the details of the system you are reviewing
  • view an organisation’s self-assessment
  • give your response to each IGP statement
  • provide comments where the organisation has identified alternative controls or exemptions for IGPs
  • write a summary for each contributing outcome
  • make recommendations for contributing outcomes
  • comment on areas of good practice for each CAF objective
  • suggest areas for improvement for each CAF objective
  • make recommendations for each CAF objective
  • comment on the quality of the organisation’s self-assessment and supporting evidence
  • explain how and when you carried out your review

Session timeout

Your session will be ended if you are inactive for 90 minutes.
Any pages you saved earlier will be stored. We will delete your responses on pages you did not save.

Before you start

  • you must be an independent assessor contracted by an organisation
  • the organisation must have given you user rights to access their WebCAF self-assessment
