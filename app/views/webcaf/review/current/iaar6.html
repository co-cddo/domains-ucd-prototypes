Period of review

The independent assurance review was conducted between 11 November 2024 and 29 November 2024, covering all contributing outcomes across Objectives A–D.

Review method

The review combined document evaluation, interviews, technical walkthroughs and evidence verification.

  • Review of self-assessment statements and supporting artefacts
  • Workshops with system owners, architects and operational teams
  • Sampling of indicators of good practice across all outcomes
  • Verification of organisational processes and technical controls

Quality and self-assessment evidence

Overall evidence quality was assessed as moderate. Most outcomes contained sufficient detail to support claims, although several areas required clarification or additional documentation. Supporting artefacts varied in completeness across different teams, particularly within Objectives B and D.

Outcome recommendations

Areas of good practice

Reviewer comments indicate consistent strengths in:

  • Clear strategic ownership and governance
  • Strong internal audit capability for security controls
  • Mature vulnerability-handling process

Areas of improvement

  • Improve evidence documentation across system boundaries
  • Introduce structured review cycles for staff training
  • Increase monitoring coverage across legacy services
