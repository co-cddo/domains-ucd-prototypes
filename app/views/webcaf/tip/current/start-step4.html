Stage 4:Review a WebCAF self-assessment

Use this service to review an organisation’s WebCAF self-assessment against National Cyber Security Centre's Cyber Assessment Framework (CAF) (opens in new tab).

You will be able to:

  • confirm the details of the system you are reviewing
  • view an organisation’s self-assessment
  • give your response to each IGP statement
  • provide comments where the organisation has identified alternative controls or exemptions for IGPs
  • provide comments where your response is different to the organisation’s selection for IGPs
  • write a summary for each contributing outcome
  • identify risks and make recommendations for contributing outcomes
  • comment on areas of good practice and suggest areas for improvement for each CAF objective
  • comment on the quality of the organisation’s self-assessment and supporting evidence
  • explain how and when you carried out your review

Session timeout

Your session will be ended if you are inactive for 90 minutes.
Any pages you saved earlier will be stored. We will delete your responses on pages you did not save.

Before you start

You must be an independent assurance reviewer contracted by an organisation to review their WebCAF self-assessment. The organisation must have given you user rights to access the self-assessment.

Before you begin your review, you may also find it helpful to have:

  • the organisation’s scoping document to refer to
  • access to the organisation’s evidence repository
  • the stage 4 guidance GovAssure (opens in new tab) open to read

Part of GovAssure process: step by step

  1. Step 1: Stage 1: Define your organisation’s context and services

    Stage 1 of GovAssure focuses on capturing and defining an overall view of your organisation’s mission, context, and essential services.

    1. Complete the scoping document
    2. Download the Stage 1 & 2 GovAssure scoping document template

  2. Step 2: Stage 2: Identify your in-scope systems and assign Government Cyber Assessment Framework (CAF) profiles

    Stage 2 of GovAssure focuses on your organisation’s critical systems.

    1. Identify and define all of your organisation’s critical systems
    2. Decide the systems you will assess
    3. Assign CAF profiles to your systems
    4. Get your scoping document approved

  3. Step 3: Stage 3: Complete a WebCAF self-assessment

    During stage 3 of GovAssure, you will assess your systems, gather supporting evidence and complete your self-assessment against NCSC’s Cyber Assessment Framework (CAF).

    1. Sign in to WebCaf
    2. Guidance for organisations

  4. Step 4: Stage 4: Have an Independent Assurance Review

    You should procure a company to carry out an Independent Assurance Review (IAR) early in the GovAssure process.

    1. Guidance for organisations
    2. Guidance for reviewers
    3. Sign in to Review

  5. or Stage 4: Have a Peer Review

    You should talk to a Peer Reviewer about your self-assessment.

    1. Guidance for organisations
    2. Guidance for peer reviewers
    3. Sign in to Peer review

  6. Step 5: Stage 5: Targeted improvement plan

    During stage 5 of GovAssure, your organisation will complete a Targeted Improvement Plan (TIP).

    1. You are currently viewing: Sign in to create your Targeted Improvement Plan (TIP)
