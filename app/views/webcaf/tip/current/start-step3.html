Stage 3: Complete a WebCAF self-assessment

The current GovAssure WebCAF is based on version 3.2 of the National Cyber Security Centre's Cyber Assessment Framework (CAF) (opens in new tab).

Providing evidence

{{ govukWarningText({ text: "You do not need to add links to your evidence in this version of WebCAF. " }) }}

You must create a separate repository to store evidence securely and share links to it. Your reviewer will need access to this repository in stage 4 in order to check your self-assessment responses.

There is more about creating an evidence repository at Stage 3 Provide your CAF self-assessment.

Sessiout timeout warning

If you are inactive for 90 minutes during a session, you will be timed out to protect your security. If you have already saved pages, these will be stored securely in WebCAF.

If you are timed out while working on a page, anything you have done will not be stored and you will have to start that page again.

Before you start

You must have completed stage 1 and stage 2 of GovAssure. This means you will have:

  • completed your organisation’s scoping document
  • identified all your systems for assessment
  • chosen the CAF profile of each system being assessed (Baseline or Enhanced)
  • nominated a GovAssure lead in your organisation, to complete the self-assessment
  • created a repository for your evidence

Part of GovAssure process: step by step

  1. Step 1: Stage 1: Define your organisation’s context and services

    Stage 1 of GovAssure focuses on capturing and defining an overall view of your organisation’s mission, context, and essential services.

    1. Complete the scoping document
    2. Download the Stage 1 & 2 GovAssure scoping document template

  2. Step 2: Stage 2: Identify your in-scope systems and assign Government Cyber Assessment Framework (CAF) profiles

    Stage 2 of GovAssure focuses on your organisation’s critical systems.

    1. Identify and define all of your organisation’s critical systems
    2. Decide the systems you will assess
    3. Assign CAF profiles to your systems
    4. Get your scoping document approved

  3. Step 3: Stage 3: Complete a WebCAF self-assessment

    During stage 3 of GovAssure, you will assess your systems, gather supporting evidence and complete your self-assessment against NCSC’s Cyber Assessment Framework (CAF).

    1. Sign in to WebCaf
    2. Guidance for organisations

  4. Step 4: Stage 4: Have an Independent Assurance Review

    You should procure a company to carry out an Independent Assurance Review (IAR) early in the GovAssure process.

    1. Guidance for organisations
    2. Guidance for reviewers
    3. Sign in to Review

  5. or Stage 4: Have a Peer Review

    You should talk to a Peer Reviewer about your self-assessment.

    1. Guidance for organisations
    2. Guidance for peer reviewers
    3. Sign in to Peer review a WebCAF self-assessment

  6. Step 5: Stage 5: Targeted improvement plan

    During stage 5 of GovAssure, your organisation will complete a Targeted Improvement Plan (TIP).

    1. You are currently viewing: Sign in to create your Targeted Improvement Plan (TIP)
