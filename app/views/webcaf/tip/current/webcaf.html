GovAssure process: step by step

The GovAssure process is made up of 5 stages, which must be completed in the following order.

  1. Step 1: Stage 1: Define your organisation’s context and services

    Stage 1 of GovAssure focuses on capturing and defining an overall view of your organisation’s mission, context, and essential services.

    1. Complete the scoping document
    2. Download the Stage 1 & 2 GovAssure scoping document template

  2. Step 2: Stage 2: Identify your in-scope systems and assign Government Cyber Assessment Framework (CAF) profiles

    Stage 2 of GovAssure focuses on your organisation’s critical systems.

    1. Identify and define all of your organisation’s critical systems
    2. Decide the systems you will assess
    3. Assign CAF profiles to your systems
    4. Get your scoping document approved

  3. Step 3: Stage 3: Complete a WebCAF self-assessment

    During stage 3 of GovAssure, you will assess your systems, gather supporting evidence and complete your self-assessment against NCSC’s Cyber Assessment Framework (CAF).

    1. Guidance for organisations
    2. Sign in to WebCAF

  4. Step 4: Stage 4: Have an Independent Assurance Review

    You should procure a company to carry out an Independent Assurance Review (IAR) early in the GovAssure process.

    1. Guidance for organisations
    1. ! Warning If you are an Independent Assurance Reviewer, you must use the WebCAF platform to review a self-assessment.
    2. Guidance for reviewers
      3. Sign in to Review

  5. or Stage 4: Have a Peer Review

    You should talk to a Peer Reviewer about your self-assessment.

    1. Guidance for organisations
      2. ! Warning If you are Peer Reviewer, you must use the WebCAF platform to review a self-assessment.
    2. Guidance for Peer Reviewers
    3. Sign in to Peer review

  6. Step 5: Stage 5: Targeted improvement plan

    During stage 5 of GovAssure, your organisation will complete a Targeted Improvement Plan (TIP).

    1. Sign in to create your Targeted Improvement Plan (TIP)
