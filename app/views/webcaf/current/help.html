This version of WebCAF is no longer in use. Organisations taking part in GovAssure cannot use this version to complete CAF submissions for the financial year 25 to 26.

Use the latest version of the Complete a WebCAF self-assessment service.

What happens to our CAF returns following submission?

Following completion of each CAF return, you will be presented with the option to download a file for your organisation's records, and store in-line with your organisation's risk appetite.

Owing to the aggregate risk of multiple Government organisations using WebCAF, we will transfer the CAF return to a SECRET classification two weeks from submission.

The data will then not be available to view through WebCAF, however progress and limited metadata will be visible to show what was submitted and when for your records.
GSG will hold these returns for data analysis at SECRET level, and further edits will not be possible.
