Please write a contributing outcome summary

How to write a good summary
GovAssure stage 4 assessments are reviewed against the contributing outcomes.

While the focus is on meeting the contributing outcome, any gaps or limitations in the cyber security measures for the contributing outcome should also be detailed in this summary.

You should reference the IGPs in your contributing outcome summary. This is particularly important if you have included justifications for individual IGPs.

The assessor will want your summary to:

  • explain what your organisation does
  • show how key controls and processes contributed to you achieving the contributing outcome
  • have brief descriptions of how these are implemented
  • include clear references to supporting evidence

Before submission, check that your overall contributing outcome achievement status and your responses to IGP statements are consistent, as the assessor will compare these. Any inconsistencies must be explained through your justifications or when changing the outcome.

For more detailed guidance on what is expected for each contributing outcome, please visit security.gov.uk.
